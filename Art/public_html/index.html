Art is a diverse range of human activities where you express your imagination. The expression or application of human creative skill and imagination, typically in a visual form such as painting or sculpture, producing works to be appreciated primarily for their beauty or emotional power.

Types of art:

  • Paintings:

    The first paintings were discovered in South West France in the Lascaux caves from the Upper Paleolithic Age, around 40,000 to 10,000 years ago.

  • Photography:

    A photograph is an image created by the exposure of light on a light-sensitive material at some stage during its making.

  • Drawings:

    As with paintings, drawings are one of the oldest art forms around and can be traced back to prehistoric times.

  • Design:

    Part of applied arts, design comes in many forms, from graphic, fashion, interior, functional to the industrial

  • New Media:

    New Media is an art form which came about in the 1960s, as artists started to experiment with developing technology in their art-making
